About Us

To facilitate the donation and delivery of items to shelters and community centres, while reducing waste in the city.

About

SimplyDonate is a non-profit organization that aims to facilitate the collection of items to be delivered to shelters and people in need.

Impressum

We aspire to become the largest non-profit geared to facilitate the donation of items to shelters and people in need.

Overview

Moving soon? Have leftover items that you're considering throwing away. SimplyDonate will come pick-up those items and donate them to local homeless shelters
